About This Portfolio

Course Overview

This portfolio showcases my work in Security & Risk Management, covering essential topics including information security frameworks, risk assessment methodologies, compliance standards, and security governance.

Through practical assignments and theoretical analysis, I've developed competencies in identifying security threats, evaluating risks, and implementing appropriate controls.

Learning Objectives

  • ✓ Understand and apply security frameworks (ISO 27001, NIST, CIS)
  • ✓ Conduct comprehensive risk assessments and threat modeling
  • ✓ Implement security controls and governance structures
  • ✓ Ensure compliance with regulatory requirements

Reflections

Personal insights and learning journey

Module 1 Reflection

Learning about security frameworks opened my eyes to the structured approach organizations must take to protect their assets. The comparison between ISO 27001 and NIST CSF was particularly enlightening, as it showed how different frameworks can complement each other rather than compete.

October 2024

Module 2 Reflection

Risk assessment proved to be more complex than I initially thought. The challenge wasn't just identifying risks, but properly evaluating their likelihood and impact. The threat modeling exercise using STRIDE methodology was particularly valuable in understanding how attackers think.

December 2024

Overall Course Reflection

This course has fundamentally changed how I view organizational security. I now understand that effective security management requires balancing technical controls with business objectives, regulatory compliance, and human factors. The practical exercises and case studies helped bridge the gap between theory and real-world application.

February 2025
